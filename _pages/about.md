# Studying how platforms and AI shape public discourse.

Haohang (**Otto**) Xin is a Ph.D. student in Media, Technology, and Society at Northwestern University.
His work sits at the intersection of political communication, computational social science, and AI-mediated information environments.

[View research](/research/){: .btn .btn--primary}
[Open CV](/cv/){: .btn .btn--inverse}

## Current work

- Mapping **digital propaganda ecosystems** across platforms.
- Measuring **polarization and echo-chamber dynamics** in high-conflict discussions.
- Building **multimodal pipelines** for text, image, and video analysis.
- Evaluating how **LLM systems** moderate, reframe, or intensify contentious narratives.
